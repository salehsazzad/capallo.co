How It's Funded

A dedicated risk reserve from our profits

Inspired by Bangladesh's bank Liquidity Coverage Ratio (10–20%), Capallo maintains a dedicated risk reserve funded from our own revenues — not investor capital. Our goal is to maintain near 100% coverage for every active investment.

Our mitigation strategy: We only work with experienced founders running high cash-flow businesses. We visit headquarters. We review 2 years of financials. We interview the team. We talk with their existing investors & takeholders. We check the bank details to understand the financial health. By the time a deal reaches you, it has already passed our internal investment approval. Most applications are declined. ALl the investments are structured as either private equity participation or Shariah-compliant arrangements — never interest-bearing instruments.
