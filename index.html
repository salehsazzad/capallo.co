ABOUT CAPALLO

Building Bangladesh's Venture Infrastructure

Capallo operates with the discipline and governance of a venture capital fund, aspiring to evolve into a professional fund manager serving the 180 million people of Bangladesh.

Our Mission

We exist to bridge the gap between institutional-grade investors and Bangladesh's most promising founders. Our goal is to democratize access to venture capital while maintaining the highest standards of governance, transparency, and investor protection.

Our Approach

We don't chase speculative 100x returns. Instead, we focus on:

  • Cash-flow generating businesses
  • Capital-efficient operations
  • Responsible, committed founders
  • Clear exit pathways
  • Strong financial stewardship
