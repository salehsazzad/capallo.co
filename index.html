LEGAL DISCLOSURE

Risk Disclaimer

Please read this carefully before investing through Capallo.

Investment Risks

  • High Risk of Loss: Private market investments are highly speculative and carry substantial risk. You may lose your entire investment.
  • Illiquidity: Investments typically require 5-7+ years before exit opportunities arise. Your capital will be locked in.
  • No Guaranteed Returns: Past performance does not guarantee future results. Most startups fail.
  • Market Risk: Our investor protection covers fraud only, not market failures or business risks.
  • Dilution: Your ownership percentage may decrease in future funding rounds.
  • Regulatory Risk: Bangladesh's regulatory environment for venture capital is evolving and may change.

Investor Protection Limitations

Capallo's investor protection framework covers only documented cases of:

  • Financial embezzlement or misappropriation of funds
  • Fabricated or fraudulent financial statements
  • Founder abandonment with capital flight out of Bangladesh

Protection is paid at the prevailing Bangladesh bank savings rate (currently ~2-4% annually) for documented fraud only. We do NOT protect against legitimate business failures, poor management decisions, market downturns, or founders who tried in good faith but couldn't succeed.

  • Not Financial Advice: Nothing on this website constitutes investment, legal, or tax advice. Consult qualified professionals.
