5. Investor Protection Framework

5.1 Scope of Protection

Capallo's investor protection covers documented cases of founder fraud only, specifically:

  • Financial Embezzlement: Misappropriation of investor funds for non-business purposes
  • Fraudulent Financials: Deliberately fabricated or materially false financial statements
  • Capital Flight: Founder abandonment with funds withdrawn from Bangladesh

5.2 What Is NOT Protected

The following are NOT covered by investor protection:

  • Market failures and business risks undertaken in good faith
  • Poor management decisions or strategic errors
  • Competitive pressures or economic downturns
  • Regulatory changes affecting the business
  • Founders who tried diligently but couldn't achieve product-market fit
  • Normal startup failure risks (which are high)

5.3 Protection Mechanism

  • Coverage Amount: Return of principal at prevailing Bangladesh bank savings rate (currently ~2-4% annually)
  • Funding Source: Risk fund set aside from Capallo's profits (targeting near 100% coverage)
  • Claims Process: Investors must provide documented evidence of fraud; Capallo investigates and determines eligibility
  • Payout Timeline: Typically 60-90 days from claim approval
  • Legal Action: Capallo reserves the right to pursue legal remedies against fraudulent founders

5.4 Limitations

  • Protection applies only to investments made through Capallo's platform with signed agreements
  • Fund availability is subject to Capallo's financial capacity
  • In extreme scenarios of multiple simultaneous frauds, payout may be prorated
  • Protection does not extend to investments made outside Capallo's facilitation
