Venture School

Venture School bridges the gap in Bangladesh's startup ecosystem by nurturing both sides of the table — investors and founders. Through two dedicated labs, we provide the space, knowledge, and community needed to fuel entrepreneurial growth as we're on a mission to back 100 high-integrity founders & empower 10,000 angel investors in Bangladesh.

Investor Lab

Conscious Investing, Made Simple

Investor Lab is Bangladesh's first structured program to help professionals, HNWIs, and aspiring angels gain confidence and skills to invest in startups.

Program Highlights:

  • Weekend classes: Friday & Saturday, 1.5 hours each
  • 8 sessions per month, cohort-based learning
  • Hands-on practice: pitch reviews & due diligence on real startups
  • Final pitch-ready session with actual companies raising funds
  • Small cohorts: only 5–7 participants per batch
  • In-person networking sessions
  • Lifetime alumni community via exclusive WhatsApp groups
  • Paid program: serious learning with measurable outcomes
Why Join? Smarter investors create better startups, and better startups transform economies.
Register for Investor Lab

Founder Lab

A Second Home for Builders

Founder Lab is where early-stage founders find the community, focus, and support to grow their ventures.

Program Highlights:

  • Flexible workspace options: hotdesks to private offices
  • Access to experienced advisors and industry mentors
  • Complimentary Capallo advisory support for strategy & fundraising
  • Learn from founders who've built successful companies
  • Connect and co-build with fellow entrepreneurs
  • Grant fund facility for eligible companies (coming soon)
  • Access to Investor Lab sessions and mentoring
Why Join? Founders don't just need a desk. They need a network, structured support system, and a community to thrive and that's our playbook to create 100 high-integrity, high-trust, high-value founders.
