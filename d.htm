Wakalah Investment Management

Shariah-compliant investment management through the Wakalah model

What is Wakalah?

Wakalah is an Islamic contract where you appoint us as your agent (Wakil) to invest on your behalf according to Shariah principles. This model ensures transparency, ethical investing, and alignment with Islamic values while pursuing competitive returns.

Our Wakalah Services

  • Shariah Screening: All investments vetted by qualified Shariah scholars
  • Ethical Investment Selection: Focus on businesses that benefit society and avoid prohibited sectors
  • Transparent Fee Structure: Clear, upfront fees with no hidden charges
  • Regular Reporting: Detailed updates on portfolio performance and Shariah compliance
  • Zakat Calculation: Assistance with determining Zakat obligations on investments

Investment Focus

We focus on halal sectors including technology, healthcare, education, manufacturing, and agriculture. All investments avoid interest-based financing, gambling, alcohol, and other prohibited activities.

Minimum Investment

Our Wakalah services are available for investors with a minimum commitment of BDT 50 lakh, allowing us to build a properly diversified portfolio while maintaining Shariah compliance.

About Capallo

Building the future of venture investing in Bangladesh

Capallo was founded with a singular mission: to democratize access to venture investing in Bangladesh and create a thriving ecosystem of informed angel investors and successful founders.
