WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

4-Week Intensive Curriculum

From deal sourcing to portfolio management, build a complete investment practice.

1

Deal Evaluation Fundamentals

Learn how professional investors assess opportunities and build your evaluation framework.

  • → Financial model analysis (beyond P&L)
  • → Unit economics (CAC, LTV, margins)
  • → Market sizing for Bangladesh (TAM/SAM/SOM)
  • → Founder assessment frameworks
  • → Business model viability tests
  • → Competitive analysis techniques
Live Exercise: Evaluate 3 real Bangladesh startups and present investment recommendations
2

Investment Structures & Term Sheets

Master the mechanics of how venture deals are structured and documented.

  • → Equity vs SAFE vs Convertible Notes
  • → Shariah-compliant structures (Mudarabah)
  • → Valuation methodologies (Berkus, VC method)
  • → Cap table dynamics & dilution math
  • → Term sheet clause-by-clause breakdown
  • → Protective provisions & investor rights
Live Exercise: Negotiate a mock term sheet with founder role-play scenarios
3

Due Diligence & Risk Management

Build systematic processes to identify risks and validate assumptions.

  • → Financial DD (red flags in statements)
  • → Legal DD (incorporation, IP, contracts)
  • → Operational DD (customer validation)
  • → Reference checks & background verification
  • → Building your investment thesis
  • → Portfolio construction strategies
Live Exercise: Complete full due diligence on a live deal from Capallo's pipeline
4

Deal Execution & Portfolio Management

From LOI to close, plus ongoing value creation and exit planning.

  • → Legal documentation (SSA, SHA)
  • → Investor onboarding process
  • → Post-investment value addition
  • → Portfolio monitoring & reporting
  • → Exit strategies (M&A, secondary, IPO)
  • → Building deal flow networks
Demo Day: Evaluate live founder pitches, make investment decisions, present rationale
